Purpose:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Demonstrate how to use ．DAC・ to play audio file..
    (1) Playing audio data from a ．Audio.bin・ file.
    
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Operation:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    (1) Connect headphone to HPO pin on target board.
    (2) Compiled to execute
    (3) Program test procedure -
		1. Press 'Enter' to play audio of 8000Hz.
		2. After playing, message will show "0. Change DAC clock frequency" or "1. Exit".
		3. After selecting '0', message will show change sample rate options(0~3); If seleting '1', system will be exited demo.
